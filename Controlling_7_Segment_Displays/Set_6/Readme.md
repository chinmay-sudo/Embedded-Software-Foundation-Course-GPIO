# Set 6

## Overview

This directory contains ten problems demonstrating advanced 7-segment display control on the ATmega2560 microcontroller (Arduino Mega). All programs use direct register access without Arduino helper functions. Each problem includes a Wokwi simulation link.

## Files

- Source files: `ETALVIS_7_Seg_Set_6_Problem_*.c`
- README: `Readme.md`

## Problems

1. Connect a Common Cathode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY number "0 to "9" one by one with delay of 1 sec  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451229298448155649

2. Connect a Common Anode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY number "0" to "9" one by one with delay of 1 sec  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451231008706177025

3. Connect a Common Cathode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY number "9" to "0" one by one with delay of 1 sec  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451231399330123777

4. Connect a Common Anode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY number "0" to "9" with decimal point one by one with delay of 1 sec  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451231320182556673

5. Connect a Common Anode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY number "0 to "9" one by one with delay of 1 sec & Reverse  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451232047495890945

6. Connect a Common Anode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY odd numbers one by one with delay of 1 sec  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451232965163552769

7. Connect a Common Anode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY even numbers one by one with delay of 1 sec  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451233316661990401

8. Connect a Common Anode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY odd numbers from 1 to 9, one by one with delay of 1 sec and display even numbers from 8 to with delay of 1 sec  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451233429103953921

9. Connect a Common Anode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY the following alphabets one by one A,B,C,D,E,F,G,H,I,J,L,O,P,Q,S,U,Y  
   Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451233790678146049

10. Connect a Common Anode 7 Segment Display on PORTA. DISPLAY the single digit prime numbers one by one  
    Simulation: https://wokwi.com/projects/451234704138901505
